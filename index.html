About Us

About Our Journey and Heritage

Devak Foods Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in the food industry. Serving the nation since last 2 decades Devak Foods has a wide presence in various states like Maharashtra, UP, Delhi, Gujrat, Rajasthan, Manipur, Meghalaya, Uttarakhand, Assam, West Bengal, M.P, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamilnadu, and other states of India.

Devak Foods Pvt. Ltd. offers an array of Ghee, Khakra, Rasgulla, Gulabjamun and other FMCG products. Exceptional Quality, Amazing Taste, Advanced Technology and Yearlong trust are few pillars of Devak Foods Pvt. Ltd. With a vision to grow and expand in every corner of India and outside India, Devak Foods is ready to meet the requirements of our consumers.
